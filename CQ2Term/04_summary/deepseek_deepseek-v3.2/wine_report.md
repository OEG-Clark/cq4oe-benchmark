## Step 5 — CQ Coverage

For each gold CQ, coverage is computed over its gold terms (classes and properties). A term is counted as covered only when its aligned pred term appears in the pred CQ with the same CQ id. This enforces CQ-local correctness.

Alignment is performed globally at the term level with a 1-to-1 mapping across systems. Coverage then projects this mapping back onto each CQ, so the evaluation separates vocabulary alignment from CQ-specific term assignment.
